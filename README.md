# Superstore Sales Analysis & Forecast – Power BI

## Project Overview
This Power BI project analyzes Superstore sales data to deliver **actionable business insights** and **short-term sales forecasting**.  
It combines historical sales analysis with **time series techniques** and interactive dashboards to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Dashboard 1: Sales Performance Analysis
- Key KPIs: Sales, Profit, Quantity, Avg Delivery Time  
- Region-wise and state-wise sales & profit analysis  
- Category and sub-category performance  
- Monthly Sales & Profit **YoY trends**  
- Customer segment, payment mode, and shipping mode analysis  

**Outcome:** Helps identify top-performing regions, products, and customer segments.

---

## Dashboard 2: Sales Forecast (15 Days)
- Time series-based **15-day sales forecast**  
- Historical sales trend analysis (2019–2020)  
- Forecast confidence band for uncertainty  
- State-wise sales contribution ranking  

**Outcome:** Supports short-term planning for inventory, logistics, and sales strategy.

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Power Query  
- DAX  
- Time Series Analysis  

---

## Business Value
- Combines **descriptive and predictive analytics**
- Enables accurate short-term sales forecasting
- Drives better planning and business decisions through interactive dashboards
